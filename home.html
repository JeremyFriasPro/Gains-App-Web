HOW IT WORKS

Click through the three ‘user’ types of Gains App.

  1. 1. Easy to sign up, set up a wallet and create your Gains App profile.
  2. 2. Select a challenge to compete against others for a chance to win cash.
  3. 3. Get ready to stream live and compete.
  4. 4. Cash payout goes directly into your wallet if you’re the winner.
  5. 5. Find another competition to participate in.
